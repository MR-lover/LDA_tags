故音乐之本，如同山川，互生互利，和于天下。 
“山川筝重奏”前身为“三川古筝三重奏”，成立于2008年，由中国音乐学院三位在校生组成。德国作曲家老锣Robert Zollitsch为其创作了许多音乐作品，确立了三川的风格和路线。在三川的音乐中，将传统的中国独奏乐器，以三重奏的形式组合在一起，结合中西方文化和创作手法，使得惯用单旋律表达的乐器顿时立体，给古老的中国乐器带来了全新的性格和生命力。三川自成立以来一直活跃在国内外音乐舞台，以不做作的音乐方向，填补了古筝音乐在当下的一个空缺。
更名后的“山川筝重奏”将继续前行。不背叛传统，不迎合浮华，代表新的民乐声音，代表年轻的中国面孔立足于世界舞台。 
SHAN CHUAN (mountians and rivers), formerly known as SAN CHUAN (Three Rivers) is a trio of three young and energetic women playing the Chinese zither Zheng. The trio has been founded autumn 2008 in Beijing by three students of the China Conservatory of Music in Beijing . Even though being very young as an ensemble, the trio has already captured a central position in the scene of Chinese contemporary chamber music. Their success is based on their musical sensitivity, instrumental mastery and most over on the unique combination of three zheng and the exquisite repertoire.
the music of SANCHUAN was mainly created by contemporary music composer Robert Zollitsch, an acclaimed expert in the realms of new Chinese art music, and who established music the foundation of SHANCHUAN. But after rename themselves into SHANCHUAN.
Maintianing their music foundation and exploring new creation, the trio renamed themselves into SHANCHUAN in 2014 and starting to establish their own status among the international music scene.
