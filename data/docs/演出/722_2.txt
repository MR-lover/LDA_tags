15187	THE HONKY TONK DEMONS	时间/Time: 2015年2月12日 21:00 门票/Ticket: 40(door)/30(presale)地点/Venue: 黄昏黎明聚乐部 (DDC) 地址/ADR: 北京市东城区美术馆后街山老胡同14号 Shanlao Hutong NO. 14, Dongcheng District 电话/Tel: 010-64078969 邮箱/Mail: DuskDawnClub@gmail.com Beijing-based Americana and Alt-Country superstars Randy Abel (The Randy Abel Stable) and Kirk Kenney (Hutong Yellow Weasels, Honky Tonk Angels) head-up a new project in the raw, bringing classic favorites and brand-spankin-new originals to the folk-hungry masses!喜欢民谣的朋友们，北京和美式相结合风格的另类乡村大师Randy Abel (The Randy Abel Stable)与Kirk Kenney (Hutong Yellow Weasels, Honky Tonk Angels) 组成了一个全新的乐队，为你们带来备受喜爱的经典曲目和原创新曲。An accomplished old-time fiddler and singer-songwriter in his own right, Kirk’s jazz-fused guitar licks back high-lonesome harmonies from the deep hollows of the Appalachian mountains, while Randy’s harmonica blasts blues from out of the deepest nowhere, and he croons fresh lyrics from some fractured place you knew by heart all the while, down deep.Kirk，一位古老的吟游者和作曲人，以他独有的吉他弹奏从阿巴拉契亚深处的幽谷中唤回幽静与和谐。Randy的口琴唤醒那些未名深处的蓝调，轻哼着那些你心底早已明了的歌词。Randy & Kirk last took the stage together when their respective bands collaborated at MIDI’s 2014 Folk Festival in Suzhou, and their groups have long been in demand on the festival, one-off and livehouse circuit from Beijing to Shanghai to Dali, Yunnan, where The Honky Tonk Demons will commence touring during Spring Festival, 2015.Randy和Kirk最后一次在舞台上的合作是在2014苏州迷笛音乐节，他们的乐队也一直在从北京到大理进行不断的演出。2015春节期间，The Honky Tonk Demons将着手在云南进行他们的巡回演出。Rounding out the paired-down line-up are Travis Klingberg, veteran Americana-bluegrass banjo-picking demon, and Ewan MacDonald, sweetly-Scots fiddler with whom the Devil seems to have gone down to Georgia at some point.Moreover these blue cats swing, and softly, with a mountain-country punch that’ll knock you straight-up and onto your happy-dancin feet, guaranteed!这些来自山地乡村的轻柔曲调必将直扣你的心扉，让你不由自主地摇摆轻舞！
