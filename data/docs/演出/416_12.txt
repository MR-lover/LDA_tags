The BigBadMantet is one of the latest collaborative jazz projects between musicians in Beijing and Shanghai that puts a modern and hip twist to familiar songs old and new: this time, they bring whacky and funky arrangements of the music of Motown, from Stevie to Smokey. The BigBadMantet is comprised of Terry Hsieh on trombone, Nathaniel Gao on saxophone, Sean Higgins on piano, Bei Bei on drums and Danny Zanker on bass. 
大大坏人爵士乐队是北京和上海几位爵士乐手的首次合作。风格在于又有独特又好听的历史中的歌曲：此时编了美国底特律60年代以下的流行音乐：从Stevie Wonder到Marvin Gaye。
