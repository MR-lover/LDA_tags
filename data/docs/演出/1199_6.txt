伴随着最新合辑“Wired”的发布，GoaProductions隆重地踢开了2015年的大门。8首充满分量的曲子，分别来自Atoned Splendor, Audioform, Black Noise, Braincell, Ecliptic, Ingrained Instincts, Predators, Solar Spectrum. 
这是一个值得庆祝的光荣时刻，我们将在北京、上海和昆明为这张合辑举行发布派对。你会听到每一首新的音乐，见证它们在舞池的无比杀伤力。 
做好准备吧，这是一场声音的冒险。无论是深邃的律动，强烈的氛围，还是动听的Riff段落，都会在Wired中呈现。 
迷幻的舞池见！ 
To kick off 2015 with a bang, GoaProductions will be releasing their latest VA Compilation “Wired”. Featuring 8 tracks of seriously Psychedelic dance music from Atoned Splendor, Audioform, Black Noise, Braincell, Ecliptic, Ingrained Instincts, Predators, and Solar Spectrum. 
In honor of this monumental occasion we’ll be holding a series of release parties in Beijing, Shanghai, and Kunming. You’ll have the chance to hear all of the tracks from the album in person as they tear up the dance-floor around you. 
Get ready for a rip-roaring adventure through a multitude of Psychedelic sounds. From deep & chunky grooves to hypnotic atmospheres, mesmerizing riffs and pounding rhythms, Wired has them all.
