6620	Toastmaster CHIC regular meeting	CHIC represents Creativity, Humor, Imagination Club. CHIC is a Toastmaster club founded by Hubert Lin -  a professional trainer with many years IT industry and training experiences who aims to help Chinese youth to better develop themselves. CHIC is a more than English or presentation club, CHIC is a warm family full of love. CHIC will provide you a wide stage to show your inner side and you will definitely have fun every Wednesday night in CHIC.
