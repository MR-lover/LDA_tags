The idea behind Too Much Shit On The Table is quite simple, there will literally be too much shit on the table. There will be various combinations of synthesizers, samplers, and effects units covering the tables. Oh, and let's not forget the the wires connecting all these things together.  All of these instruments will be used to make different types of electronic music.
Too Much Shit On The Table背后的点子特别简单，就是因为“桌上屎太多了”。桌子上将会布满了合成器、效果器、采样什么的。还有就是千万别忘了把这玩意都特么连接起来。这些乱七八糟的玩意都是用来做各种电子的。
