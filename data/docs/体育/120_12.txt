棒球是美国最大体育项目之一，你是个棒球迷吗？你知道美国棒球的种族界限是自非裔棒球手Jackie Robinson与布鲁克林道奇棒球队签约了1946年赛季才打破的吗？想了解更多这项运动的历史以及废除种族隔离吗？
作为我们黑人历史月活动的一部分，北京美国中心自豪奉献电影“42号传奇”，影片讲述了Jackie Robinson的真实故事，导演布莱恩•海尔格兰德，主演查德维克•博斯曼。影片重点放在Jackie Robinson不但改变了棒球而且还加速了公民权利运动。
影片放映后将有短暂的提问回答环节。
本活动向公众开放，不包括媒体。媒体如要采访北京美国中心的活动，须提前与美国使馆新闻处取得联系。
This program will be in English; the film will have English subtitles. 
Are you a fan of baseball, one of the most popular sports in the United States? Did you know that the “color line” in American baseball was broken when African American Jackie Robinson signed with the Brooklyn Dodgers for the 1946 season? Want to explore more about the history and desegregation of this sport?
As part of our Black History month programming, the Beijing American Center proudly presents a screening of “42”, the real life story of Jackie Robinson.  Directed by Brian Helgeland and starring Chadwick Boseman , the film depicts how Jackie Robinson not only changed baseball but hastened the Civil Rights Movement. 
There will be a short Q&A in English after the movie. 
This event is open to the public, not open press. Press inquiries about BAC events should be directed to the U.S. Embassy Press Office.
